# Let's Build Something Together 🚀

Hi there! 👋

We're **Collaborate Solutions**, a software development team looking to build long-term partnerships with reliable, friendly, and open-minded people.

Interested in building your own software collaboration group, agency, or company—but don't have the time to build and manage a full development team?

**That's where we can work together.**

## 🤝 The Idea Is Simple

You can build or grow your own software business while our experienced team handles everything.

You focus on the partnership, ideas, and business growth. We can support:

* Project sourcing and planning
* Technical project teams
* Full-stack development
* Frontend and backend engineering
* Cloud and DevOps
* AI and automation
* Technical discussions and project meetings
* Project delivery and ongoing support

**You bring the partnership. We bring the technical team. Together, we grow.**

## ⏰ Flexible Around Your Schedule

Already have a full-time job or your own business? No problem.

Your involvement can be flexible. Depending on the partnership and client agreements, our team can handle much of the development, technical communication, and day-to-day project work.

You can stay focused on your current priorities while still helping build a long-term software business.

## 💰 Grow Together

As a partner, you may receive **20%–40% of the partnership revenue**, depending on your role and contribution.

For successful ongoing collaborations, potential partner revenue may range from approximately **$3,000–$8,000 per month**, depending on active projects and actual business revenue.

There are no guarantees—our goal is to build real projects, strong client relationships, and sustainable revenue together.

## 🙌 Who We'd Love to Meet

We're looking for people who are:

* Reliable and trustworthy
* Friendly and open-minded
* Professional and responsible
* Interested in long-term collaboration

You don't need to handle every technical task. Software or business experience is helpful, but finding the right long-term partner matters most to us.
